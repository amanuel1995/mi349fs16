  • What is eco-fashion or Green Fashion? It is a part of the growing design philosophy and trend of sustainability, the goal of which is to create a system which can be supported indefinitely in terms of human impact on the environment and social responsibility. It can be seen as an alternative trend against fast fashion.


  • What materials do you usually recycle? Water bottles, plastic containers, Aluminium cans, used up earbuds cables, and all other recyclable stuff.


  • Where is this start-up based on? This start-up is based on Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. An East African country that is known for peculiar art, fashion, and music.
